        A list of dictionaries, corresponding to the input_data list, with each

        'pile': [4.649586613302491, -0.1269025023692098, -0.08186831350347822, 0.0021372473711799705],

        for k in _COEFFS:

        beta = next(iter(_COEFFS.values()))

    import math
