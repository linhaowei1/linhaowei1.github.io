Haowei Lin(林昊苇)

I am a senior undergraduate majoring in Artificial Intelligence at Yuanpei College in Peking University. I am a member of Tong Class, which is a study program of Artificial Intelligence led by Professor Songchun Zhu.

I am fortunate to work as a research assistant in Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology under the guidance of Prof. Bing Liu. My research interests lie within many fields, including Open-world Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing and Computational Biology.

Previously, I also had two fantastic intern experiences. I have worked as an NLP research intern with Dr.Zilong Zheng and Dr.Wenjuan Han in Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI) and an engineering intern with Douyin Search group in ByteDance, Beijing.
