I am a senior undergraduate majoring in Artificial Intelligence at Yuanpei College in [Peking University](https://english.pku.edu.cn/). I am a member of [Tong Class](http://www.ai.pku.edu.cn/info/1086/1887.htm), which is a study program of Artificial Intelligence led by Professor [Songchun Zhu](http://www.stat.ucla.edu/~sczhu/). 

I am fortunate to work as a research assistant in [Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology](https://www.icst.pku.edu.cn/english/home/index.htm) under the guidance of Prof. [Bing Liu](https://www.cs.uic.edu/~liub/). My research interests lie within many fields, including Open-world Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing and Computational Biology.

Previously, I also had two fantastic intern experiences. I have worked as an NLP research intern with Dr.[Zilong Zheng](https://zilongzheng.github.io/) and Dr.[Wenjuan Han](http://hanwenjuan.com/) in Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence ([BIGAI](https://www.bigai.ai/)) and an engineering intern with Douyin Search group in [ByteDance](https://www.bytedance.com/en/), Beijing.
