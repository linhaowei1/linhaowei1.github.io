I am a senior undergraduate majoring in Artificial Intelligence at Yuanpei College at [Peking University](https://english.pku.edu.cn/). I am a member of [Tong Class](http://www.ai.pku.edu.cn/info/1086/1887.htm), which is an elite undergraduate program in Artificial Intelligence led by Professor [Songchun Zhu](http://www.stat.ucla.edu/~sczhu/). 

I am fortunate to work as a research assistant at [Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology](https://www.icst.pku.edu.cn/english/home/index.htm) under the guidance of Prof. [Bing Liu](https://www.cs.uic.edu/~liub/). Previously, I also had two fantastic intern experiences. I have worked as an NLP research intern with Dr.[Zilong Zheng](https://zilongzheng.github.io/) and Dr.[Wenjuan Han](http://hanwenjuan.com/) at Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence ([BIGAI](https://www.bigai.ai/)) and as an engineering intern with Douyin Search group in [ByteDance](https://www.bytedance.com/en/), Beijing.

This fall, I will begin my Ph.D. studies at the Institute for Artificial Intelligence, School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Peking University, advised by [Prof. Jianzhu Ma](https://majianzhu.com/) and [Prof. Yitao Liang](http://web.cs.ucla.edu/~yliang/). My research focuses on the intersection of machine learning and computational biology. I'm also interested in open-world machine learning and NLP.
